#Corona Cases Finder - SUMIT SAURAV

You can find corona cases in india state-wise by this application.
Following data you will get to know:-
a) Total Confirmed Cases
b) Total Active Cases
c) Total Recoveries
d) Total Deaths
e) Total Cases of districts of selected state.

#Platfrom:
Python 3.8.2

#Modules:
1.Tkinter
2.requests

#Requirment:
a) Internet Connection
b) Window PC

#Data Site:
https://api.covid19india.org/
